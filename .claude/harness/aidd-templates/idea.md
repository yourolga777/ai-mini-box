# Idea

> AIDD layer 1 — the seed. One paragraph, plain language. Edit freely; this
> file never expires. AI agents read it before suggesting features.

## Problem

What real-world frustration exists today, for whom, and why current tools
fall short? Be specific about the user, the moment of pain, and the cost
of putting up with it.

## Hypothesis

What you believe a tool/product/service in this space *could* do that would
materially change behaviour. One sentence. If you can't fit it in one
sentence, the idea is two ideas — split.

## Non-goals

What this idea is NOT trying to be. Listing the rejected adjacencies is
how you keep the agent from wandering into them.
